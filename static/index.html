Scoring Types

Standard

A fixed number of points are awarded based on how correct your answer is. Full points for a correct answer, partial points for a close answer, and no points for an incorrect answer.

Wager

You choose how many points to put on the line (up to the question's maximum). The outcome determines what happens to your wager:

ResultPoints
✅ Correct+wager (you gain your wager amount)
〰️ Partial±0 (you break even)
❌ Incorrect−wager (you lose your wager amount)

Bonus points are added to your total score and reflected on the leaderboard once an admin records the outcome.

How to Play

Submitting Your Rankings

Before the season starts, rank all contestants from #1 (your predicted winner) to #N (your predicted first boot). Once the first elimination is recorded after you submit, your rankings are locked and you earn points as castaways are voted out.

If you haven't submitted rankings yet and the season has already started, you can still submit — but you won't earn points for contestants who have already been eliminated or removed. Those picks will be marked as Late pick in your scores. All remaining contestants are scored normally.

!! SPOILERS !!

Eliminations for weeks 1 and 2 will be posted on the Tuesday before week 3 airs. After that, eliminations will be posted on the Tuesday following each episode.

Removed Contestants

Occasionally a castaway is pulled from the game for medical or other reasons rather than being voted out. When a contestant is marked as Removed, no points are awarded for that castaway regardless of how you ranked them. They will show as "Removed" in your score breakdown.

Example: Aubrey is voted out 1st, Joe is removed 2nd, Tiffany is voted out 3rd. If you ranked Tiffany 22nd (anticipating Joe's departure would push her to slot 3) or 23rd (simply predicting she'd be the 2nd voted out) — both earn a perfect 10 pts.

Quick Examples

You ranked Cirie Fields at #3. She finishes 4th (not in the final 3) — her finish position is 4. You're off by 1, earning 9 points. Standard scoring always applies for non-Final-3 castaways, even when you had them in your top 3.

You ranked Ozzy Lusth at #2. He makes the final 3 but doesn't win — you had him in your top 3, earning 10 points.

You ranked Rick Devens at #1. He wins the season — perfect pick, earning 20 points.
